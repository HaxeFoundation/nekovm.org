Getting in touch

Have some questions? Want to get involved in Neko development?

The Neko Community welcomes you at Github or at the community forum. You can ask questions, report bugs/issues and contribute to the source.

Neko is a product of the Haxe Foundation.

The Haxe Foundation was created to fund long term Haxe and Neko related development and provide support to companies.

You can support us by making a donation. The Haxe Foundation also offers Paid Support Plans.
